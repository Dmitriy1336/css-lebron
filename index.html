LeBron James

An American basketball player who plays as a light and heavy forward

Early life

James is 206 cm tall and weighs 113 kg. He played most of his career as a light forward and heavy forward, but could also be involved in other positions when needed. His style of play, athletic and versatile, caused James to be compared to Hall of Fame members Oscar Robertson, Magic Johnson and Michael Jordan. As of June 2019, James' career stats are averaging 27.2 points, 7.4 rebounds, 7.2 assists, 1.6 steals and 0.8 blocked shots per game. Since 2011, LeBron James has been recognized as the best NBA player by ESPN and Sports Illustrated.
